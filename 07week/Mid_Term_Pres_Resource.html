Note that if you have a different paper that aligns with one of the areas above that you wish to present, you need to ask for instructor permission. For each of the topics above, prepare a presentation which will include:

  • Several slides
  • Prerecorded demonstration videos of the method mixed within your presentation
  • The presentation should be at least 15 minutes long, but no more than 20 minutes, and will include:
    • motivation and background
    • related academic work
    • explanation of the method, with algorithm, psedo-code and sample of the results with video and/or imagery
    • limitations of the proposed approach
  • Solo presentations or groups or more than 2 are not allowed except by instructor permission
  • See presentation videos Links to an external site. from recent years for an example.

It is important to note that the video should not be all at the end of the presentation.
